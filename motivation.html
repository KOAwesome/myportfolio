Drive to move forward

Today I was watching a telugu movie called Eagle. It is a action film with many unrealistic scenes. I thought it was waste of time but still watched cause to kill time. I learnt two things from this movie.

  • I heard this quote like Only the matured people can actually see the real truths hidden in life. Like depending on the knowledge of the reader the message conveyed could be completely different.
  • After watching the movie I was super motivated like till then I was literally doing nothing. So I thought to do some work. In the movie the hero goes to extremes, always learning new things improving.. etc. And look how it is in Software Industry I remember watching a reel and laughing to it. A robber comes to rob a big company he points his gun at software developer he doesn't know anything then the gun is pointed to database programmer and it just keeps on changing. I also feel life is too fleeting gotta enjoy it before it goes.
